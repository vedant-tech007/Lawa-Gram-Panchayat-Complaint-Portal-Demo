Welcome to Lawa Gram Panchayat

Welcome to the official website of Lawa Gram Panchayat. This portal is created to connect villagers with Panchayat services in a simple and transparent way. Our aim is to provide important information, solve public complaints quickly, and support the overall development of the village through digital technology.

Lawa Gram Panchayat continuously works for the welfare of the people by improving village infrastructure, sanitation, water supply, roads, streetlights, and public services. Through this website, villagers can stay informed about government schemes, village meetings, notices, and ongoing development projects.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a clean, educated, digitally connected, and self-reliant village where every citizen gets equal opportunities and better public services. We aim to encourage transparency, public participation, and sustainable rural development. Lawa Gram Panchayat dreams of creating a modern village that preserves its cultural values while adopting new technology for faster communication and improved administration. We want every villager to have access to quality education, healthcare, sanitation, clean drinking water, proper roads, and a safe environment. Our goal is to strengthen unity among villagers and motivate citizens to actively participate in village development activities. Through teamwork and responsible governance, we aim to make Lawa village a model village for future generations.

Staff Members

The Staff Members page provides information about the officials and employees responsible for managing and resolving village complaints through the Village Complaint Management System. This section helps villagers identify the concerned staff members, their roles, and responsibilities within the Gram Panchayat administration. The page is designed to improve transparency, accountability, and communication between citizens and local authorities. Each staff member is assigned specific duties related to public services such as water supply management, sanitation, electricity maintenance, road development, health services, and complaint monitoring. Villagers can view staff details including names, designations, departments, and contact information to better understand whom to approach for specific issues. This system ensures smoother coordination and faster resolution of complaints while supporting efficient village administration and community development.
